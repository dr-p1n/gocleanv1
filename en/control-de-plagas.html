Pest Control

An unresolved pest problem is a legal, health, and reputational risk all at once.

In Panama, the tropical climate accelerates pest proliferation year-round. For a restaurant, hotel, or food plant, a single pest report can lead to a temporary MINSA closure, client lawsuits, or irreparable damage on social media. Pest control isn't reactive — it's a preventive system that protects your operating license.

What we cover

  • Certified fumigation for commercial and industrial facilities
  • Preventive control on a recurring schedule (monthly, bimonthly, quarterly)
  • Control of rodents, crawling insects, flying insects, and termites
  • Documentation of every intervention for sanitary audits

Backed by ISO 9001 certification, ISSA and APAFAM membership, with certified products and specialized technical staff across 9 provinces.
